# Business Memo

## Purpose

Summarize the findings from the churn analysis and recommend actions for business stakeholders.

## Executive summary

- Objective: identify customers at risk of churn and support retention decisions.
- Approach: analyze customer behavior, support tickets, web activity, and past interventions.
- Outcome: model and data insights to drive retention strategies.

## Key insights

- Customer segments with highest churn risk
- Most important features driving churn predictions
- Data limitations and quality considerations

## Recommendations

- Prioritize outreach for high-risk customers
- Improve customer support response and intervention timing
- Track data quality and update models regularly

## Next steps

- Validate model predictions with business outcomes
- Develop targeted retention campaigns
- Expand data collection for ongoing churn monitoring
